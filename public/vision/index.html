Our Journey

From a single greenhouse
to a 100-hectare vision

2018

First seeds planted. A 5,000 m² lettuce greenhouse. No investors, no playbook. Just the conviction that Paraguay deserved better food.

2023

Marca País certification. International recognition. Our tomatoes, peppers, and lettuces in 50+ B2B accounts. Proof that hydroponic quality sells.

2025

1 hectare high-tech greenhouse. Hoogendoorn climate computers. Kritifil diffusion film. Automated ventilation. Mid-tech Dutch standard — top-tier for Latin America.

2026

AgroPark Villeta. 100 hectares secured. 2 new hectares under construction. AI-powered operations. European investor partnerships. The platform is ready to scale.

2030

The vision. 20 hectares under Kritifil premium film. Export to Brazil, Argentina, Bolivia. Latin America's reference greenhouse operation. Growing 4 hectares per year.
